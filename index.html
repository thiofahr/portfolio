01 — About

Hi, I'm Thio.

I'm a physics graduate from Universitas Padjadjaran with interest in climate dynamics, energy transition, and science communication. My research at BRIN examined equatorial wave energy patterns during the 2023/24 El Niño event using FFT-EOF methods. I've also contributed to the energy modelling effort of IESR as a research intern, along with methane emission analysis from coal and oil & gas sectors in Indonesia.

I'm drawn to problems where physical science, data analysis, and real-world decision-making meet, whether that's reducing uncertainty in climate projections, modeling energy systems, or translating research into forms that communities can act on.

Alongside research, I've been a member of Pajajaran Physical Society since 2023, writing about physics and climate, running science outreach for high school students, and now serving on the Board of Directors. I also co-wrote a climate change book for children and contributed to a patent of a photobioreactor for carbon capture.
